Classic Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons melted butter

For the filling:

  • 32 oz (4 packages) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  3. Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth and creamy.
  4. Add sour cream and vanilla extract, mix well.
  5. Add eggs one at a time, beating on low speed after each addition.
  6. Pour filling over the crust.
  7. Bake for 55-60 minutes until the center is almost set.
  8. Cool completely, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
